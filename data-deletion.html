Palm - Data Deletion Instructions

To request deletion of your data from Palm, please follow these steps:

  1. Log into your Palm account
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Click on "Delete My Data"
  4. Confirm your decision

Alternatively, you can email us at badlaniaditya@gmail.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request"

We will process your request within 30 days and notify you once completed.
